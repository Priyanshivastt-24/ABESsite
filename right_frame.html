The library at ABES Engineering College is a center for academic resources and learning, offering an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital materials across various engineering and management disciplines. Equipped with modern facilities, it provides a conducive environment for study, research, and collaboration. With access to digital databases, e-resources, and knowledgeable staff, the library supports students and faculty in their pursuit of knowledge and academic excellence. Key features of the ABES Engineering College Library include:

  1. Comprehensive Collection: Over 50,000 volumes of books, covering diverse subjects in engineering, science, and humanities.
  2. Digital Resources: Access to prominent e-resources like IEEE, J-Gate, and other leading databases.
  3. Well-Equipped Reading Rooms: Spacious and comfortable seating for individual study as well as group discussions.
  4. Supportive Staff: A team of knowledgeable librarians and staff members who assist students in navigating resources and finding relevant information.
